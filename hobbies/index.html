Where words end, music begins.

Music has always been more than just a hobby — it's a core part of who I am. I find joy in singing, writing songs, and crafting melodies that capture emotions words alone can't. Whether it's a quiet late-night idea or a tune stuck in my head all day, I love turning thoughts into music that resonates. Creating original songs allows me to tell stories, reflect on experiences, and connect with people in a way nothing else can. It's where creativity flows freely, and I feel most like myself.

Between the Lines

I read to slow down. To shift perspective. To listen in on lives that aren't mine.
Books give me space to think; and sometimes, to not think at all.
They’re quiet company on busy days. A reset when the world feels loud.

Some stories challenge me. Others comfort me.
But all of them, in some way, help me understand the world, and myself — a little better.
