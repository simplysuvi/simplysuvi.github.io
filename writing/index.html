Image-Based Machine Learning in Automotive Used Parts Identification for Remanufacturing

Islam, A., Jain, S., Nenadic, N.G., Thurston, M.G., Greenberg, J., Moss, B.

Published: 26 January 2024

DOI: 10.1002/9781394214297.ch39

Co-authored a research chapter presenting a computer vision and machine learning framework for automated identification of used automotive parts in remanufacturing. My work focused on developing YOLO-based detection, Inception-based classification, and multi-camera decision fusion methods to achieve over 95% identification accuracy, reducing manual sorting and improving remanufacturing efficiency for sustainable production.

Contaminant Investigation and Pre‐Processing Opportunities for Textile‐To‐Textile Recycling

R. Parsons, S. Jain, A. Islam, M. Walluk, and M. Thurston

Published: August 2025

DOI: 10.1002/amp2.70034

Co-authored a peer-reviewed study that applies machine learning and computer vision to automate contaminant detection and material classification in post-consumer garments. My contributions focused on developing deep learning models for garment classification, trim detection, and segmentation of recyclable materials—advancing scalable, AI-driven textile recycling workflows for a circular economy.
